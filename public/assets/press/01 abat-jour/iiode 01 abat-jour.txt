iiode collaborations 01
Abat-Jour

iiode is a lighting brand making bright, caring products. In 2026, we launch Re27: a serviceable E27 LED bulb made from recycled materials, offering natural light quality and gentle smart control. Repairable by design and made in Switzerland with EU-made parts, Re27 updates existing luminaires and enhances new fixtures. Lighting made conscious.

At Matter and Shape 2026, iiode presents a collaborative exhibition centred on the Re27 light bulb. Eight invited designers/studios have developed lampshades for a shared Re27 base — Dimitri Bähler, BIG-GAME, Jörg Boner, From Lighting, Alexandra Gerber, Vanessa Schindler, Charlotte Talbot & Jonathan Mauloubier, Panter & Tourron — released as playful limited editions exploring materials and pendant-light uses.
